Dogecoin
Categories Info
Name: DogeCoin
Definition/Summary: Dogecoin is a cryptocurrency created by Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer on December 6, 2013, who made it originally as a joke, being known as a "meme coin". It recently gained popularity over the years, but it now sits at less than a dollar.
Value: 23.14 PHP
Ethereum
Categories Info
Name: Ethereum
Definition/Summary: Ether is the second highest cryptocurrency in terms of market capitalization, only behind Bitcoin. It was officially launched on July 30, 2015. It is the currency on a platform called "Ethereum" where users are able to create transactions with items such as "NFTs".
Value: 198,428.21 PHP
Bitcoin
Categories Info
Name: Bitcoin
Definition/Summary: Bitcoin, released in 2008 by Satoshi Nakamoto, an anyonymous person, is the posterchild of all the cryptocurrenies and has the highest value of all the cryptocurrenies along with the highest market share. No cryptocurrency is more known than Bitcoin.
Value: 5,813,410.41 PHP
